Features

Digital Employee is the next generation of business-customer interactions. Our robust, intelligent system is capable of solving the most complex problems that your business face. Some of our features include...

Expert Intelligence

Our intelligent expert system dissects natural language questions to answer any inquiry your customer might have about your business or products and provides them with the answers they need

Integrated Upselling

Based on your customers' inquiry, Digital Employee will automatically recommend the best product for their needs or promote current sales to ensure that your customers have the best shopping experience possible

Product Item Location

The capability to provide the exact location of items and services to everyone in the building including the ability to text instore maps to inquirers guaranteeing your customers always find what they are looking for

Employee Connection System

Certain conditions require the presence of physical assistance such as getting items of top shells and unlocking items in secured displays. When a customer needs physical assistance Digital employee will notify the nearest employee through your companies preferred medium

Data Analytics

Optimize your business with new data analytics on customers behavior and trends to create better products and services and increase profits

Constantly Improving

When Digital Employee isn’t able to answer a question our system delegates the question to an in store employee, meanwhile our back end system stores the query and the system can be updated in under 24 hours
